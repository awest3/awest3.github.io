About

Anton H. Westveld

I am a senior lecturer (Australian system) in the Research School of Finance, Actuarial Studies & Statistics at the Australian National University and an affiliate associate professor in the Department of Statistical Sciences and Operations Research at Virginia Commonwealth University.

Curriculum Vitae (follow embedded links to research articles)

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Statistics at the University of Washington in Seattle where I was also a member of the Center for Statistics and the Social Sciences. My research interests include Bayesian methodology and theory, network and relational data, game theoretic data, inference (uncertainty quantification) for agent based stochastic computer simulation models, and statistical causality.
